How to Play

Goal: Connect the Start Anime to the Target Anime using shared Voice Actors.

  1. Click on a Voice Actor (Seiyuu) from the current anime's cast.
  2. You will see a list of other anime they have voiced in.
  3. Click on an Anime to move to it.
  4. Repeat until you reach the Target Anime!

Tip: Use the "Show Hint" button if you get stuck, but it has a cooldown!

Note: Some Chinese (Donghua) or Korean anime may not have Japanese Voice Actors listed. If you get stuck on one in JP mode, you may need to restart or reroll.
